# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Importers, distributors, retail buyers and e-commerce sellers in Korea, Japan, Europe and North America who need dependable product sourcing and long-term supply-chain coordination in China.

## Product Purpose

Present Y&Z Global as a trusted China sourcing and supply-chain partner that reduces risk, speeds up decisions, controls quality and coordinates suppliers, warehousing, shipping and after-sales work. The primary conversion goal is a qualified project inquiry with a product image, link or specification.

## Brand Personality

Decisive, operational, dependable. The experience should create confidence and momentum without making empty best-price or best-quality claims.

## Anti-references

Generic freight-forwarder websites, commodity export templates, bubbly SaaS card grids, decorative glassmorphism, beige editorial landing pages, vague best-price language and cluttered dashboards.

## Design Principles

- Show control through a visible, understandable process.
- Lead with importer outcomes rather than a list of services.
- Use cinematic scale for the promise and quiet clarity for the proof.
- Make Korea and Japan expertise concrete through certification and packaging details.
- Keep every conversion path fast, direct and easy to trust.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA contrast, keyboard-operable navigation and forms, visible focus states, responsive layouts down to 375px and reduced-motion alternatives.
